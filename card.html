Ninetales

Ninetales is a fox-like pokemon with sinister red eyes which gives it the ability to psychically control the minds of people and other Pokémon.

  • Name: Ninetales
  • Species: Fox Pokémon
  • Evolved From: Vulpix
  • Abilities: Flash Fire

This fox-like Pokémon possesses nine tails, hence its name. Ninetales have high intelligence and strong loyalty to their owners. They can live for a thousand years when they use the power of their tails.
